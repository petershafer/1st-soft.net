About Us

Welcome to the Coffee Corner, a shrine to coffee.

People know coffee by many names, but few know the history, processes, politics, health benefits and recipes behind one of the world's favorite drinks. With this site, our hope is to inform people about coffee and promote a great local coffeehouse, The Artist's Cup Café.
