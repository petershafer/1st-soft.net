About Us

The Coffee Corner was put together by six Allegheny College students for Professor Ann Bomberger's Technical Writing course.

The Team:

Rachel Atchley (contact: atchler@allegheny.edu) is a Junior and a Neuroscience and Psychology major. She loves coffee in every form, so long as it is sweet and strong. She's been a Barista at the Artist's Cup Café for over four years. She drinks White Mochas like nobody's business. Rachel wrote the Drinks and Interviews sections, designed the website, and took the photographs.

Lydia Gearhart (contact: gearhal@allegheny.edu) is currently a Sophomore who suffers from the plight of the undergrad: declaring a major. Though she is currently undecided, she embraces late night study sessions, 8 a.m.s, and Red Eyes to help her through both! Lydia wrote the Fair Trade section.

Michele Harris (contact: harrism@allegheny.edu) is a Junior and a English and Creative Writing major. While she usually drinks coffee to sustain late-night paper writings, she does enjoy its flavor on a chilly winter morning. Her favorites are Breakfast Blend, French Roast, and Café au Lait. Michele co-wrote the History section.

Greg Holland (contact: hollang@allegheny.edu) is a Senior and a Creative Writing major who enjoys caffiene for late-night writing sessions. Not one for fancy mixed drinks, Greg likes his coffee strong and black, with only a little sugar. Greg co-wrote the History section.

Katharine Platt (contact: plattk@allegheny.edu) is a Junior and a Biology major. Although she would prefer hot chocolate to coffee, she gained a new appreciation for coffee and coffee lovers while creating this website. Katharine wrote the Health section.

Erin Vandeveer (contact: vandeve@allegheny.edu) is a Junior and a Communication Arts major. Her favorite coffee concoctions are Caramel Macchiatos and chocolate-covered espresso beans. Erin wrote the Cultivation section.

Thanks for your interest in our site.
